Pyramid Technical Institute (PTI) is a non-governmental organization committed to reducing unemployment and enhancing livelihood opportunities in the Sunsari district of Province No. 1, Nepal.

Founded in 2011, we deliver CTEVT-affiliated technical and vocational training to youth, women, and marginalized communities — equipping them with skills for dignified careers and sustainable livelihoods.

🎯

Our Mission

Deliver accessible, quality technical education to build a skilled workforce in eastern Nepal.

🌱

Our Vision

A self-reliant community where every individual has skills to lead a dignified life.

🤝

Inclusivity

Special focus on women, Dalit, Janajati, and marginalized youth for equitable access.

🏆

Quality

Industry-aligned, practical hands-on training with CTEVT-certified instructors.

How to Enroll

1

Submit Application

Fill out the online form or visit our Itahari office with your citizenship copy and 2 passport photos.

2

Orientation Interview

Attend a brief orientation where our team explains program details, schedule, and requirements.

3

Fee & Documentation

Complete formalities. Many programs are fully subsidized for eligible candidates through partner NGO funding.

4

Begin Training

Start on the batch date. Receive materials, toolkit (select programs), and daily stipend if eligible.

📌 Free Programs Available: Several programs are free or heavily subsidized for women, Dalit, Janajati, persons with disabilities, and economically disadvantaged youth through our partner organizations.
